Solutions

Learning Tapestry builds and operates infrastructure components with new capabilities on top of existing education systems.

These components help provide snap-in digital content search and analytics functions in learning environments.

These resources are released as open source to provide low-cost and modifiable software to plug into today’s content and learning management systems.

Learning Content Management System (LCMS)
Our LCMS engine enables organizations to author full-course curricula and instructional materials efficiently using Google Docs.

Google Drive Bulk Downloader
Our Bulk Downloader tool makes it easy to create editable copies of many files and folder and place them in the same organized way in another Google Drive account.

Open Content Exchange (OCX)
Our open metadata language allows organizations to more effectively describe their curricular content so that it can be more easily moved from one platform to another.".

AI Content Generation
We operate a leading-edge AI content generation platform, primarily to create knowledge building content for children. Contact us for a demo.
